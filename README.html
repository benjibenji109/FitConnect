Purpose of the Project FitConnect is a simple web application designed to help users manage their fitness journey. The platform allows users to track workouts, monitor progress, and connect with trainers in one web application. It improves accessibility and helps users stay organized and motivated toward their fitness goals. How to Navigate through FitConnect index.html: First begin by opening the index.html page where you will be greeted by the initial design of the web application. From here you will either want to press Login on the top right if you already have an account, or you can press "Get Started" to register. Press get started. login.html: Pressing "Get Started" will bring you to the "login.html" page where you can either input your email/username and password if you already have an account, or you can press "Sign up" right below which will take you to the "register.html" page. Press "Sign up". register.html: In the register page you will have the option between choosing to be a trainer or a user. Once you choose, you can input your personal information and click create account which will direct you to the "dashboard.html" page. dashboard.html: Once on the "dashboard.html" page you can view your entire fitness overview for the week and different information on your account. We can start by clicking Workouts which will take you to the "workouts.html" page. workouts.html: In the "workouts.html" page you can view your workouts planned for the week and see what each workout has in store. Next, click on Progress which will take you to the "progress.html" page. progress.html: In the "progress.html" page you are able to view different class activity as well as your goal tracker. Next, click on Trainers which will take you to the "trainers.html" page. trainers.html: In the "trainers.html" page you are able to view different trainers and their fortes. Once you pick a trainer you can message them (feature not yet implemented). You can also search for a specific trainer to find one you're looking for. Now click on Profile which will take you to the "profile.html" page. profile.html: Your profile page is where you can see all your personal information. It is not fully developed yet (you cannot edit information), but it is meant to show your age, weight, height, and overall goals. This page also shows who your current trainer is and gives you the option to view more trainers if needed.
